2.2 Linking to Equations

Below is an important equation with a label:

\(\seteqnumber{0}{}{0}\)

\begin{equation} a^2 + b^2 = c^2 \label {eq:pythagoras} \end{equation}

We can refer to it as Equation (1) anywhere in the text. This uses amsmath for equation handling.

We can also use other math environments, e.g. align:

\(\seteqnumber{0}{}{1}\)

\begin{align} \nabla \cdot \vec {E} &= \frac {\rho }{\varepsilon _0}, \label {eq:maxwell1} \\ \nabla \cdot \vec {B} &= 0, \label {eq:maxwell2} \\ \nabla \times \vec {E} &= -\frac {\partial \vec {B}}{\partial t}, \label {eq:maxwell3} \\ \nabla \times \vec {B} &= \mu _0 \vec {J} + \mu _0 \varepsilon _0 \frac {\partial \vec {E}}{\partial t}. \label {eq:maxwell4} \end{align}

Equations (2)–(5) show Maxwell’s equations.

4 More Equation Samples (amsmath)

Here are some additional equation samples using amsmath.

4.1 Summations and Integrals
\(\seteqnumber{0}{}{5}\)

\begin{equation} \sum _{n=1}^{\infty } \frac {1}{n^2} = \frac {\pi ^2}{6}. \end{equation}

\(\seteqnumber{0}{}{6}\)

\begin{equation} \int _{0}^{1} x^2 \, dx = \left [\frac {x^3}{3}\right ]_0^1 = \frac {1}{3}. \end{equation}

\begin{equation} f(x) = \begin{cases} x^2, & \text {if } x \ge 0, \\ -x, & \text {if } x < 0. \end {cases} \end{equation}
